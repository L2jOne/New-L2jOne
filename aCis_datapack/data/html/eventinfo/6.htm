Simon Says
Event Information
The goal is to say the exact text as the NPC says as fast as possible.
If player didn't say the text within 10 seconds he's out.
If player said the text wrong he's out.
If player said the text last he's out.
Red nick means you're out.
Green nick means you said the text correctly.
All players are paralyzed during the event.
The winner is the last person to stay with green nick.
